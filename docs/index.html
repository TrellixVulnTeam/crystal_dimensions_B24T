About the games designer

I am a highly talented games designer. My inspiration to make this game was just the idea of being able to play a game I created the concept for.

My influences in writing the story line for this game were various Marvel productions, like the film Avengers End Game. Their superhuman characters have super powers like in crystal dimensions. Also, Marvel story lines usually end in the saviour of the Universe.
